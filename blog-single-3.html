Solution Partner Program Relaunch

Overview: This case study explores the preparation for the relaunch of the Solution Partner Program.

Objectives:

  • Craft partner-facing content to support the relaunch of the Solution Partner Program.
  • Deliver clear and understandable program updates explanation.
  • Follow up with current partners to update their business and product information, remove dormant partners.

Approach:

  • Conducted wellness check for current partners to verify their business status.
  • Sent check-in emails and termination letter to inactive partners.
  • Wrote the program update content for email and website channels.

Results:

  • Found 60+ dormant partners and terminated them from the program, maintaining the program's quality.
  • Updated the program website and email template to reflect the new program updates.
