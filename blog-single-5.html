Anytime, Anywhere: Introducing the New Solution

Overview

A short-form video promotion series for SOLIDWORKS xDesign — targeting the EDU student community on Instagram to drive product awareness and boost sales.

Objectives
  • Create a series of videos to promote SOLIDWORKS xDesign to the EDU audience.
  • Boost awareness and sales of SOLIDWORKS xDesign.
  • Engage with the student and educator community on Instagram.
Approach
  • Drafted video scripts based on product features, benefits, and student use cases.
  • Shot and edited the short-form videos for Instagram.
  • Published posts and monitored engagement to optimize content cadence.
Results
✦ 5,000+ likes received across the Instagram video series.
✦ Product sales surpassed the Q3 2024 target.
