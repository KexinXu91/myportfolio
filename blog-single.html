Merchandising Strategy for a Passionate Community

Overview: This case study explores the development and execution of a merchandising strategy tailored for a highly engaged user community.

Objectives:

  • Understand the needs, preferences, and purchasing behaviors of the community.
  • Design and launch a line of merchandise that reflects the community's identity and aligns with the company's branding.
  • Coordinate the RFP process, manage the vendor relationships, and ensure to launch the e-commerce website on time.

Approach:

  • To better understand the community, I conducted a market research based on the community past activities, online forums, and social media posts. I also conducted an online survey that invited the community to share their thoughts and preferences for the merchandise.The survey gained 100+ responses, which helped the team to understand what products the coommunity is interested in.
  • By collaborating with the procurement team, we successfully sourced a vendor that could produce the merchandise and build storefront at a competitive price. During the process, I was responsible for drafting the RFP file, organizing questionaire answers, and rating the scorecard which contributed to the final decision.
  • Considered this is an e-commerce website, I focused on the digital marketing which can direct the traffic and boost the sales. During the campaign, I created content for 4 channels: blog, social media, email, and forum. I also worked with the IT team to build the website and ensure it is user-friendly and optimized for SEO.

Results:

  • The e-commerce storefront will be launched in Q4, 2025 and a special pop-up event will be held in Q1, 2026.
