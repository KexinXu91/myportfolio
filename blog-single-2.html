Improving Email Marketing Performance

Overview

A data-driven audit and improvement initiative for email marketing campaigns — using performance analysis, hypothesis testing, and A/B testing to lift open and click-through rates.

Objectives
  • Understand current email performance patterns and identify key factors impacting open and click-through rates.
  • Develop a structured improvement plan based on the analysis.
  • A/B test improvements and measure their impact on campaign performance.
Approach
  • Collected and analyzed 100+ past email metrics using a structured model to systematically identify performance patterns based on defined criteria.
  • Developed targeted hypotheses focusing on subject lines, sender names, preheader text, audience segmentation, and send times.
  • Designed and executed A/B tests to isolate and validate the impact of individual variables across different audience segments.
  • Analyzed results to determine the most effective variables and refined the improvement plan; collaborated with IT to update the email template.
Results
✦ Email open rate increased by 15% after the improvement plan was implemented.
✦ Audience segmentation framework adopted as standard practice for ongoing campaigns.
