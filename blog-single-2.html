Improve the Email Marketing Performance

Overview: This case study explores the performance of the email marketing campaign and the improvement plan.

Objectives:

  • Understand the current email performance patterns and identify the key factors that impact the email open rate and click-through rate.
  • Conclude the improvement plan based on the analysis.
  • A/B test the improvement plan and measure the impact on the email performance.

Approach:

  • Collected and analyzed 100+ past email metrics using a model to systematically rate and identify performance patterns based on defined criteria.
  • Developed targeted hypotheses derived from the analysis, focusing on variables such as subject lines, sender names, preheader text, audience segmentation, and send times.
  • Designed and executed a series of A/B tests to isolate and validate the impact of individual variables on email open rates across different audience segments.
  • Analyzed the results of the A/B tests to determine the most effective variables and refine the improvement plan.
  • Collaborated with the IT team to update the email template.

Results:

  • After the improvement plan was implemented, the email open rate increased by 15%.
