Behind the Design: Customer Storytelling Series

Overview

A customer storytelling series produced for SOLIDWORKS EDU — short-form video interviews published on Instagram to humanize the brand, showcase real student and professional stories, and drive community engagement.

Objectives
  • Create a series of customer stories to showcase the value of SOLIDWORKS products and services.
  • Share stories on Instagram and other channels to increase brand visibility and credibility.
  • Engage with the customer and educator community.
Approach
  • Researched current customers' businesses and active projects to identify compelling story angles.
  • Contacted interviewees via email and coordinated interview scheduling and logistics.
  • Shot, edited, and posted short videos as teasers for the full blog stories on Instagram.
Results
✦ 17 stories published, reaching a wide audience and strengthening brand credibility and community engagement.
✦ Content averaged 2,000+ engagements per post across the Instagram series.
